    KNOW THE COUNTRY

    Understand the Locals and their Traditions

    Before you head out, do your research on the different customs (the do's and don'ts) of the country you're traveling to. For instance, what percentage do they typically tip? Are there different laws? Are there common tourist scanms? Will there be any cultural events while you're there? There are a lot of things you might not even think of, but it's simple enough to research "do's and don'ts" infographics on different countries for that information.
